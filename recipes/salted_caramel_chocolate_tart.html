Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart

Decadence Defined: The Ultimate Salted Caramel Chocolate Tart

Indulge in a dessert that perfectly marries rich, velvety chocolate with buttery, luscious salted caramel. This tart features a crisp, buttery crust that provides the perfect foundation for layers of smooth caramel and silky dark chocolate ganache. Each bite delivers a harmonious blend of sweet and salty flavors, creating a truly unforgettable experience for your taste buds.

Crafted with care and attention to detail, this dessert is as elegant as it is indulgent. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself, the salted caramel chocolate tart is guaranteed to impress with its rich textures and sophisticated flavor balance. It’s the ultimate sweet ending that leaves a lasting impression.

Ingredients

  • For the crust:
    • 1 1/4 cups (160g) all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup (30g) powdered sugar
    • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 1-2 tbsp cold water
  • For the salted caramel:
    • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
    • 6 tbsp (85g) unsalted butter, room temp
    • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream, warmed
    • 1 tsp sea salt
  • For the chocolate ganache:
    • 8 oz (225g) dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
    • 3/4 cup (180ml) heavy cream
    • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Make the crust:
    • Combine flour and powdered sugar in a bowl.
    • Cut in the cold butter until mixture looks like coarse crumbs.
    • Add egg yolk and 1 tbsp cold water, mix gently to bring dough together (add more water if needed).
    • Press dough into a tart pan with a removable bottom. Chill for 30 mins.
    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Bake crust for 15-20 minutes until golden. Cool completely.
  2. Make salted caramel:
    • Heat sugar in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until melted and amber-colored.
    • Carefully add butter, stir until melted.
    • Slowly add warm cream, stirring until smooth.
    • Stir in sea salt. Let cool slightly.
  3. Assemble tart:
    • Pour caramel over the cooled crust, spread evenly.
    • Chill 20 minutes until set.
  4. Make chocolate ganache:
    • Heat cream until just boiling, pour over chopped chocolate.
    • Let sit 2 minutes, then stir until smooth.
    • Stir in butter until glossy.
  5. Finish tart:
    • Pour ganache over caramel layer.
    • Chill at least 2 hours or until firm.
  6. Serve:
    • Sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt on top before slicing. Enjoy!
